========================================
                GAME RULES
========================================

INTRODUCTION
------------

Your original message has been corrupted by a sequence of text transformations!
In order to successfully recover it, you must figure out which transformations 
were applied and in what order.

You know:
  • The set of possible transformations

You do NOT know:
  • The order in which transformations were applied

You have N total guesses each game

Your goal is to reverse all transformations
and recover the original message.


HOW TO PLAY
-----------

Each round:
  1. Enter the number corresponding to the
     transformation you believe was applied.
  2. The game evaluates your guess.
  3. One transformation is reversed.


SCORING
-------

If your guess was:

  • The most recent transformation applied:
        +1 point

  • Otherwise:
        +0 points


GAME END
--------

The game ends after you make N guesses.

Color Coding
------------
Each round, certain transformed characters will be color coded:
  - Red - Character was swapped
  - Blue - Word was merged
  - Green - Word was swapped
