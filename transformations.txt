
Transformation Rules:
1. Shift
    - Each word has its letters shifted by a distinct amount. 
    - Think of these shifts as moving "forward" in the alphabet by the
    shift amount.
    - ex. "Abc" shifted by = "Bcd"
2. Character swap
    - Each word has at least one character swapped with a different word
    - Space characters are not swapped (Number of words and word length are unchanged)
3. Word swap
    - Between 1 and 3 words have their positions swapped with other words
4. Word Merge
    - Between 1 and 3 words will be merged with the next word in the sequence

Other Constraints:
1. Merge-Swap rule:
    - If merging occurs before word swapping, at least one of the merged words will be
    swapped
    - If swapping occurs before merging, at least one of the swapped words will be merged
2. Each transformation is applied exactly once to the original text
